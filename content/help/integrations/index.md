## Integrations

Vanilla integrates with several third party applications, including:

* Salesforce
* Zendesk
* GitHub 
* Wordpress 
* Akismet 
* WithList 
* MailChimp 
* Social logins (Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, etc.) 

Vanilla also supports integration through an [API](../api).
